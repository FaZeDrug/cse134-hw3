Parental Concerns in Roblox - Early Research Scholars Program

  • Carried out an independent research project over the course of an academic year as part of the Early Research Scholars Program, a research experience for undergraduate students in computing/engineering.
  • Worked on "Parental Concerns in Children Gaming in Roblox" research paper under Professor Munyaka. Project entailed analyzing parental concerns about Roblox (posted on Reddit) to highlight the disparity of parental knowledge about Roblox.
  • Roblox is a popular online sandbox game among children.
  • Paper accepted to IEEE ISTAS '24.
